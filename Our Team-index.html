Authors' Biography

Sister to Sister, authors Hannah and Moricka Burgess have taken different paths; only to arrive at the same destination, with one voice, one tone, and one hunger, to write. Each individual experience over the last fifteen years prepared them for the unified sound of Seasons. These experiences include publishing in the International Library of Poetry, Long Island Chronicle, freelance writing for Adelphi University and Howard “The Hilltop” University, ranging from ethnic and social issues to plays and theatrical performances. They have written for religious institutions and enjoyed the winnings of poetry contests. They have presented themselves to the literary arena and today they write as one score.

About Moricka

About Moricka

If asked, where do I fit? I guess I’d say literary fiction with an upmarket twist of writing that blends the line between commercial and fiction. I lean toward Fiction and Poetry. Anything created from a feeling, idea or thought compels me to read on. Where did this love come from, you might ask? Nikki Giovanni's, book Love Poems showed me the powerful simplicity in writing what you feel. By the stroke of her pen, she exposed herself to the world. Her legacy of the love of words is what I aspire to find in other authors. Ntozake Shange's powerful drama Lillian has enough literary pearls- and a few hints of crazy, to keep you engaged. Some days, I’m in the mood for "laugh out loud" romantic comedies. Bridget Jones' Diary and Big Girl Panties are the two titles that come to mind. I believe in a world with so much destructive racial and political tension driven by social media, a story we read that breaks through the brokenness to remind the world to laugh; calls me. I hope that such writing can span the decades or centuries. Books that line the shelves of tiny local bookstores where everyone knows you- a community of readers. But, I don’t mind the large chains. They bring a sense of progress, and a reminder that no matter how busy our lives get, there’s a corner nook you can hide and escape into a world of words. That’s why I am a part of Worldccp. And why you should be, too. If you comb the land in search of a publisher to protect and present your work in its best light for the world to see, then search no further. Welcome home. We only choose a small number of literary trailblazers, so our focus isn't impeded. We believe in working closely with our authors to make sure we give their creative ambitions the biggest opportunity for success over the long term. There’s a story in you just like there's a story in me. While sitting here looking out of my guest room window past the green fields and birch trees, I see the lines of text, clearly. Do you?
